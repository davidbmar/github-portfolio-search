`★ Insight ─────────────────────────────────────`
- **Word-boundary regex vs. simple `in`**: Using `re.search` with boundary-like patterns prevents false positives — e.g., the word "class" inside "classification" won't match. The pattern `(?:^|[\s\-_/,;:.(])kw(?:[\s\-_/,;:.)!?]|$)` handles markdown formatting, punctuation, and hyphenated compound keywords like `api-gateway`.
- **Static method design**: `extract_topics()` is a pure function with no dependencies on store or embeddings, making it independently testable and reusable (e.g., for export scripts or CLI tools).
- **Set union merge**: `sorted(set(github_topics) | set(inferred))` ensures deterministic, deduplicated output regardless of source overlap.
